
The prince and Rapunzel meet when the prince climbs Rapunzel's hair to reach her tower. They fall in love and plan to run away together. The wicked enchantress finds out and cuts off Rapunzel's hair. Rapunzel is sent away and the prince is blinded.

A few years later, Rapunzel has a baby and she and the child live in a cottage in the woods. One day, the prince is out hunting and he hears Rapunzel singing. He follows the sound of her voice and they are reunited. His sight is restored and they live happily ever after.
